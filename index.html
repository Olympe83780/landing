What is our purpose

Do we need to have an aviation club at HEC Paris ? The answer is yes. Aviation is everywhere in our lives. No planes, no trade, no goods, no business. HECair was created to allow those who are passionate about aviation to meet, to make their networking basis bigger and to find internships. HECair's goal is also to show everyone what aviation really is, beyond preconceived ideas.

Our program

A year at HECair looks like this : conferences with actors from the aviation industry (entrepreneurs, consultants, etc.), company and airport visits, cases studies with our partner companies and networking events with HEC alumnis.
